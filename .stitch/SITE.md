# Site Documentation

## 1. Site Vision
The project is a modern, personalized gym marketing website focusing on converting visitors into members. It offers a unique BMI-based personalized experience, where diet plans, success stories, and recommendations adapt to the user's BMI category. The aesthetic is "Minimalist Industrial Fitness" prioritizing dark backgrounds, bold typography, and neon green accents.

## 2. Core Identity
- **Name:** Gym Transformation Hub
- **Tagline:** You Know Your Goal. Now Let's Know Your Path.
- **Tone:** Energetic, motivational, precise, modern.
- **Target Audience:** Beginners looking for fitness guidance, weight-loss/muscle-gain seekers, and individuals interested in joining a gym.
