# Design System

## 1. Minimalist Industrial Fitness Concept
The aesthetic embraces a stark, high-energy environment similar to a modern performance laboratory.

## 2. Colors
- **Primary Background**: `#0F0F0F` (Deep Space Dark)
- **Secondary Background**: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal Surface)
- **Accent Color 1**: `#39FF14` (Neon Green - representing growth and energy)
- **Accent Color 2**: `#FF6B00` (Electric Orange - representing intensity)
- **Text Primary**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Text Secondary**: `#B3B3B3` (Light Gray)

## 3. Typography
- **Headings**: `Bebas Neue`, `Anton`, or `Oswald` (Bold, condensed, impactful fonts)
- **Body Text**: `Inter`, `Poppins`, or `Roboto` (Clean, highly legible geometric sans-serifs)

## 4. UI Elements
- **Roundness**: Slightly rounded corners (`4px` to `8px`) or sharp corners (`0px`) for an industrial feel. Avoid fully rounded pill shapes unless it's a specific badge.
- **Shadows**: Subtle, harsh drop-shadows on cards instead of soft glows, emphasizing the "industrial" aesthetic.

## 5. Composition and Motion
- Minimal text, letting imagery (fitness models, gyms) speak.
- Generous use of white (negative) space between sections.
- Large, bold typography acting as a graphic element.

---

## 6. Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED FOR ALL PROMPTS):**
- **Color Mode:** DARK
- **Backgrounds:** Use pure dark (`#0F0F0F`) and secondary dark (`#1A1A1A`) exclusively. No light mode configurations.
- **Accents:** Use Neon Green (`#39FF14`) for primary calls to action (Join Now, Calculate BMI) and active states. Use Orange (`#FF6B00`) for warnings or secondary highlights.
- **Typography:** Headlines must be bold, condensed sans-serifs. Body copy should be clean, highly legible sans-serif. Use high contrast.
- **Layout:** Minimalist, industrial layouts. Content should breathe with ample padding.
- **Component Style:** Use sharp angles or minimal border-radius. High-contrast borders for form fields. Let large imagery and bold typography be the focal points.
